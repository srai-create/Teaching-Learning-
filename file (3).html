Week 1 — Behaviour for Learning

Creating calm, purposeful classrooms rooted in virtues and clarity.

Home Programme Overview

Core Objectives

  • Creating a calm and purposeful classroom climate.
  • Securing attention through clear expectations.
  • Embedding first‑time compliance and movement routines.
  • Managing noise and behaviour proactively.
  • Using strong lesson starts and endings.

Ethos Connection

Behaviour for Learning is framed by core Nishkam virtues: humility, service, compassion and truth. Teachers lead by modelling calmness and consistency, supporting children to regulate themselves and participate with dignity.

Coaching Focus Points

  • Clear, concise instructions using a standard script.
  • Silent signals and non‑verbal cues.
  • Positive framing and ratio of praise.
  • Consistency across the team to build shared expectations.
