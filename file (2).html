Programme Structure

Week 1 — Behaviour for Learning

Creating a calm, purposeful climate through clear routines, first‑time compliance and strong lesson starts/ends.

Week 2 — Explaining & Modelling (I Do → We Do → You Do)

Direct instruction, clear modelling, guided practice and independent application.

Week 3 — Checking for Understanding

Mini-whiteboards, scanning, visible responses and adaptive decisions based on evidence.

Week 4 — Questioning & Classroom Dialogue

Cold calling, probing, think‑pair‑share, and maintaining academic diligence.

Week 5 — Probing Questioning

Deepening thinking, addressing misconceptions and stretching understanding.

Week 6 — Adaptive Teaching & Inclusion

Designing accessible lessons, scaffolding, and proactively planning for all learners.
